Tips for Using Astrology in Everyday Life

Everyone can use a little help to make life run more smoothly. The following tips will show you how to use astrology to your advantage, and help you set your goals and objectives to correspond with the natural rhythm of everyday life.

1.Pay Attention to Mercury Retrograde

Avoid New Ventures During Retrograde

Mercury retrograde is a phenomenon that takes place about three to four times a year where it appears as if Mercury is moving backwards in the sky. It is the worst possible time to sign a contact, begin a new project, or purchase a big ticket item. When Mercury goes retrograde, the air is filled with static that causes confusion. You may lose your keys, send an email to the wrong person, or have your travel plans interrupted. In light of this confusion, don't begin anything new during this time.

Time to Take Care of Past Issues

Mercury retrograde is not all bad. This period is a wonderful time to do research, revisit old projects, or get in touch with people from your past. It's a good time to speak with former supervisors about job openings, have dinner with an old lover, or catch up on paperwork or errands that you've overlooked up until now. In short, it's a good time to take care of things from the past.
